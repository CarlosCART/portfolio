Chronoshift
Globe Travel agency is a website project I did with the help of 2 partners. The goal was to make a website where people could schedule a flight and learn about different countries, their history, attractions, and places where people can stay during their vacations. During this project I learned how to divide a project into different members. One of the most difficult challenges I had was when I was designing the footer and putting its elements in their corresponding place. Thanks to persistence and collaboration from the CART “Habits of Mind” I was able to do a great job on this project.
