Bouncy Balls
The Bouncy Balls was one of my last projects I did. It required the use of JavaScript. The goal was to make colored balls bounce automatically. Thanks to the project, I learned how to make motions with JavaScript. I was encountered with a problem while making this project, which consisted of not being able to make the balls bounce. I asked my partner for help; thanks to him I was able to get the project done. I used the Collaboration method from CART “Habits of Mind”.
